Welcome to
Arctic Breeze

Arctic Breeze Energy operates in the energy efficiency and remote control technology sector using the Internet of Things (IoT). The company specializes in the development of innovative solutions for the management and reduction of energy consumption in business, educational environments and busy locations such as schools, shopping centers and buildings.

About Us


Arctic Breeze Energy was founded in the year 2000 with the mission of addressing environmental issues through adaptable technological solutions. Over the years, we have developed various projects and now focus on offering innovative alternatives to a common contemporary challenge: excessive energy consumption. We specialize in the field of energy efficiency technology and remote control through the Internet of Things (IoT). Our solutions are tailored to businesses, educational institutions, and other high-traffic areas such as schools, shopping malls, and office buildings. Committed to innovation, sustainability, and customer service, we provide advanced tools for efficient energy management, promoting both economic savings and environmental care.
